CTRL Cohort: Empower Your Community

A guided program for students to launch real-world projects or help local businesses using CTRL's toolkits and platform.

What is CTRL Cohort?

CTRL Cohort is a hands-on program for students and aspiring changemakers to make a real impact in their community. Join a guided journey to start your own project or help a local business grow using our proven toolkits and resources.

How It Works

  • Apply to join the next CTRL Cohort (open to students and community members)
  • Get matched with a local business or start your own project
  • Access exclusive toolkits, mentorship, and step-by-step guidance
  • Collaborate, learn, and make a measurable impact
  • Showcase your work in the CTRL Spotlight

Why Join?

  • Real-world experience and leadership skills
  • Direct community impact
  • Use CTRL's launcher toolkits
  • Portfolio and resume building
  • Opportunities for recognition and awards
